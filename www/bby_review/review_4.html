Better for espresso than coffee
on July 7, 2014
Posted by: Jazzcocktails
from White Plains, NY, USA
Gender:Male
I keep this machine in my office at work and have pretty much abandoned the Keurig in the lunchroom. The machine is handsome and feels well built, although pod ejection is a bit fiddly. It is a visual/design enhancment to most offices.
You can keep it close by because the machine keeps pretty clean (w/o Aerocino) although it is a bit loud. The aroma will drive your office mates nuts - it's terrific.
I prefer the espresso to the full cup of coffee. One trick that the salesperson at 63rd and Madison showed me was to muddle the crema into the liquid below because so much of the flavor is in the crema. This particularly helped with the 8 oz. full cup.
For me, the half decaf coffee pod tastes the best of the 8 oz. varieties. Second would be the Odacio. I enjoy all of the espressos, and choose depending on mood. I hope they introduce some new varieties, as I am sure these will get better over time. Another flavor option is to add an espresso shot to the 8 oz. cup, delicious, but that gets pricey.
I also own a Nespresso Le Cube which I still enjoy but at this point I would recommend you go with the new technology and its added flexibility.
Pros: Durable, Quality, Time-Saver, Easy to Use, Speed, Design
I would recommend this to a friend!
